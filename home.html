About Merchify

Project Objectives

Provide AI-based outfit suggestions and a better user experience.

Customization

Marketplace Support

Community Engagement

Location-Based Drops

Subscription Model

Creator Marketplace

Designers can upload their own designs and earn royalty on each sale.
